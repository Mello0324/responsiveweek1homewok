About me

Front-end developer from Maseru,Lesotho

ka lebitso ke Mamello Khokoane, ke moqapi oa li-interface tsa webo, 'me ke chesehela ho qapa liphihlelo tse monate le tse bonolo ho basebelisi. Ke thabela ho fetola meralo le likhopolo hore ebe liwebsaete le lits'ebetso tse sebetsang,tse sebelisanang le basebelisi.

kena le tsebo e tebileng ea HTML,CSS le JavaScript,'me ke setsibi sa ho aha libopeho tse arabelang,ho kenya ts`ebetsong likarolo tse matla le ho ntlafatsa tsebetso ho fana ka maeto a se nang moeli ho basebelisi

Ke molateli ea tiileng oa ho ithuta khafetsa le ho lula ke ipehile boemong ba morao-rao ba theknoloji le mekhoa ea morao-rao li-interface tsa webo.

labalabela ho kenya letsoho ka litsebo tsaka leho sebelisana le lihlopha ho theha litharollo tsa lijithale tse ncha le tse nang le ts`uts`umetso.Sepheo saka keho aha mosebetsi moo nka sebelisang boitsebo baka ho ntlafatsa liphihlelo tsa basebelisi le ho kenya letsoho lefatsheng le lulang le fetoha la ntsetsopele ea webo.
